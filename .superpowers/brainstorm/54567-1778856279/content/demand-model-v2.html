Entropy & Realism Injectors

Applied per-location and per-event to prevent synthetic "too-clean" patterns

Order volume ±15% Gaussian noise per hour
Prep time Normal(μ=12min, σ=3min) carryout; Normal(μ=31min, σ=6min) delivery
SOS breach ~8% of orders; spikes to 18% during event multipliers
Cancelled orders ~2.5% rate; higher on 3PD channel
Waste events ~3% of prep volume, skewed toward end-of-day
Item price drift Quarterly ±3–6% price adjustments per menu item
Unit-level variance Each location has a ±20% persistent volume bias (high/low performers)
Staff no-shows ~4% shift no-show rate; correlates with SOS breaches
Loyalty tier drift Members progress through tiers based on cumulative spend over time
New guest growth ~0.8% net new guest profile creation per day per location
